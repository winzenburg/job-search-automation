## The Three Core Drivers

Every opportunity Ryan evaluates should hit **Strategy + Systems + AI.**

Not every principal design role fits. Use this framework to filter.

---

## Quick Evaluation (2 minutes)

### Ask These Three Questions

#### 1. **Strategy + Systems Thinking** ⭐
- Will I shape product/design strategy, or execute within existing strategy?
- Will I build frameworks/systems that scale beyond one team/product?
- Am I designing the design function (meta) or designing within it?

**YES to all 3 = ✅ Pass**  
**YES to 1-2 = ⚠️ Caution (verify in interview)**  
**NO to all 3 = ❌ Skip**

#### 2. **AI/Emerging Tech** ⭐
- Is AI mentioned in the product vision or company strategy?
- Will I work on AI-era design challenges?
- Is this forward-looking or legacy tech?

**YES = ✅ Pass**  
**Maybe = ⚠️ Caution (ask in recruiter call)**  
**NO = ❌ Skip**

#### 3. **Leverage Multiplier** ⭐
- Does my work scale (across teams/clients/products)?
- Or is it single-team execution?
- Does the role multiply my impact?

**YES (consulting, design systems, platforms) = ✅ Pass**  
**Maybe (scaling startup) = ⚠️ Caution**  
**NO (single team) = ❌ Skip**

## Scoring

| Score | Fit Level | Action |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| 3/3 | **STRONG** | Apply immediately. Top priority. |
| 2/3 | **GOOD** | Worth applying if other factors align (company, salary, location). |
| 1/3 | **WEAK** | Only apply if desperate or company is exceptional. |
| 0/3 | **SKIP** | Pass, even if fancy company or high salary. |

---

## Red Flags (Auto-Skip if Present)

❌ Design seat without strategic influence  
❌ No mention of mentoring/team leadership  
❌ Solely visual/execution focus  
❌ Maintenance of existing systems (not building new ones)  
❌ No AI/emerging tech in product vision  
❌ Single-team scope, no leverage multiplier  
❌ "Design by committee" culture signals  

---

## Green Flags (Seek These)

✅ Explicit mention of "design strategy" or "design leadership"  
✅ "Design systems" or "design operations" in title/description  
✅ "AI," "emerging tech," "evolving," or "pioneering" language  
✅ Mentoring, "establish standards," "shape practice"  
✅ Multi-team, multi-product, consulting, or platform scope  
✅ Strategy + execution (not execution-only)  
✅ "Systems thinking" or "framework" language
