## Executive & Leadership Recruiters

### Retained Search Firms (High-Touch)
- **Aquent Creative Circle** — https://aquent.com/creative-circle
  - Specializes in: Design leadership, design operations, creative direction
  - Approach: Contact directly + submit profile
  - Likely salary range: $150K+
  
- **Toptal Design Leadership** — https://www.toptal.com/designers
  - Specializes in: Top-tier design talent, leadership roles
  - Approach: Apply to network, express interest in principal roles
  - Note: Vetting process, high barrier but premium placements
  
- **Onward Search** — https://www.onwardsearch.com
  - Specializes in: Executive search, VP/C-suite, director roles
  - Approach: Contact about open searches, express availability
  
- **Robert Half Creative** — https://www.roberthalf.com/en/creative
  - Specializes in: Full-time and contract creative roles
  - Approach: Register profile, discuss opportunities with recruiter

### Approach: Direct Outreach
Send a brief message: "I'm exploring principal-level design roles and am interested in discussing [company/sector] opportunities. Here's my background: [1-line pitch]. Let's connect."

## Design Communities (Private/Hidden Opportunities)

### Tier 1: Active, Leadership-Focused
- **DesignX Community** (Slack/Discord)
  - Access: Invite-only, ask via existing members
  - Content: Job postings in #opportunities channel
  - Best for: Unpublished roles, peer referrals
  
- **Friends of Figma** (Discord + Local Meetups)
  - Access: Free to join at figma.com/community
  - Content: Member job board, #jobs channel
  - Best for: Design tool–adjacent roles, design systems
  
- **Product Design Guild** (Slack)
  - Access: Apply at productdesignguild.com
  - Content: Job postings, mentorship
  - Best for: Product-focused roles, peer network
  
- **Rosenfeld Media Community**
  - Access: https://rosenfeldsupplies.com/community
  - Content: Member forums, occasional job postings
  - Best for: UX research + strategy roles

### Strategy:
1. Join communities (most are free or low-cost)
2. Introduce yourself in #introductions
3. Watch #jobs / #opportunities / #postings channels
4. Network with members (many are hiring managers)
5. Share your work / ask for advice (builds trust, leads to referrals)

## Notes

- **Salary negotiation:** Start high ($180K+), expect to land at $165K+
- **Equity:** For startups (Wellfound), negotiate for 0.5-1.5%
- **Remote preference:** Most boards have remote filters; use them
- **Timeline:** Principal roles typically 4-8 week cycle (longer than IC roles)
- **Community value:** 30-40% of principal roles come from referrals, not postings
