## HOW TO EVALUATE OPPORTUNITIES (Updated Mar 5, 11:52 AM)

**Evaluation Framework: Does the role hit Strategy + Systems + AI?**

### Three-Point Checklist
Ask these questions about every opportunity:

1. **Strategy + Systems Thinking** ⭐
   - Will I shape product/design strategy or execute within existing strategy?
   - Will I build frameworks/systems that scale beyond one team/product?
   - Am I designing the design function or designing within it?

2. **AI/Emerging Tech** ⭐
   - Is AI mentioned in the product vision or company strategy?
   - Will I work on AI-era design challenges?
   - Is this a forward-looking or legacy tech role?

3. **Leverage Multiplier** ⭐
   - Does my work scale (across teams/clients/products)?
   - Or is it single-team execution?
   - Does the role multiply my impact?

**Scoring:**
- 3/3 = **STRONG FIT** — Apply immediately
- 2/3 = **GOOD FIT** — Worth applying if other factors align
- 1/3 or 0/3 = **LIKELY SKIP** — Even if fancy company or high salary

### Red Flags (Avoid These)
- Design seat without strategic influence
- No mention of mentoring/team leadership/framework building
- Solely visual/execution focus
- Maintenance of existing systems (not building new ones)
- No AI/emerging tech in product vision
- Single-team scope (no leverage multiplier)

### Green Flags (Seek These)
- Explicit mention of "design strategy" or "design leadership"
- "Design systems" or "design operations" in title/description
- "AI," "emerging tech," or "evolving product" language
- Mentoring, "establish standards," "shape practice"
- Multi-team, multi-product, or consulting scope
- Strategy + execution (not execution-only)

**Example High-Fit Roles:**
- Toast (Product Designer - Platform Experience): Strategy ✓, Systems ✓, AI ✓ → 3/3
- Thresh Consulting (Senior Product Designer, UX/UI Lead): Strategy ✓, Systems ✓, AI ✓ → 3/3
- Design Systems Lead at AI Company: Strategy ✓, Systems ✓, AI ✓ → 3/3

**Example Low-Fit Roles:**
- Senior Product Designer at mature fintech: Strategy ?, Systems ?, AI ? → Need to verify
- UX/UI Designer at enterprise company: Strategy ✗, Systems ✗, AI ? → Likely pass
- Design Manager at agency: Strategy ✓, Systems ?, AI ? → Depends on agency focus
