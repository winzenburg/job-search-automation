## How to Use

**LinkedIn Connect Note:**
1. On recruiter's profile, click "Connect"
2. Click "Add a note" 
3. Paste one of the messages above (fits 300-char limit)
4. Click "Send"

**After sending connect request:**
- Follow up with full message via DM (once connected)
- Or add their email and send fuller email version
