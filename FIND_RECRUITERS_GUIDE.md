# How to Find Specific Recruiters on LinkedIn

**Fastest approach:** Search LinkedIn directly for each firm's recruiters.

---

## 1. AQUENT CREATIVE CIRCLE

**LinkedIn Company:** https://www.linkedin.com/company/aquent-talent

**How to find recruiters:**
1. Go to LinkedIn
2. Search: `Aquent Creative Circle design recruiter`
3. Or visit: https://www.linkedin.com/company/aquent-talent
4. Click "People" → Filter by "Design" or "Recruitment"
5. Look for titles like:
   - Senior Recruiter, Design
   - Design Recruitment Specialist
   - Creative Talent Manager
   - Director of Design Recruitment

**Send to:** careers@aquent.com (general) or DM the specific recruiter

## QUICK SEARCH TIPS

### Method 1: Company Page → People
1. Visit company LinkedIn page
2. Click "People" tab
3. Search by keyword (design, product, recruiter)
4. Click recruiter profile → "Message"

### Method 2: LinkedIn Search Bar (Fastest)
Search box, type:
```
Design recruiter AND Aquent
```
or
```
Korn Ferry product design consultant
```

### Method 3: Filter Results
Once you search, filter by:
- Current company: [Company name]
- Job title keyword: recruiter, manager, consultant, partner
- Location: (if relevant)

## WHAT TO LOOK FOR IN RECRUITER PROFILES

✓ **Title contains:** Recruiter, Manager, Consultant, Partner, Specialist
✓ **Current company:** The recruiting firm (Aquent, Onward, etc.)
✓ **Recent activity:** Posted in last month = actively working
✓ **Specialization:** Design, product, UX, creative mentioned
✓ **Profile strength:** 500+ connections = well-networked
