## Competitive Landscape Analysis

### Category 1: Traditional Design Ops Leaders (High Volume, Low Threat)

**Profile:**
- 10-15 years design operations experience
- Strong process optimization background
- Design systems expertise
- **Gap:** Limited AI transformation experience

**Typical Background:**
- Came up through IC design → design systems → design ops
- Focused on tooling, workflow optimization, hiring
- Good at scaling existing processes
- Weak on transforming operations with AI as core capability

**Why They're Not Direct Competition:**
- Positioning themselves as process optimizers, not transformation architects
- "How do we use AI tools?" vs. "How do we transform operations with AI?"
- Incremental improvement mindset, not systemic redesign
- Missing the strategic AI fluency companies need

**Example Profiles (Generic):**
- Head of Design Ops at [SaaS Company]: 12 years experience, design systems focus
- Director of Design Operations: Workflow optimization, team scaling, tooling
- Lead Design Ops Manager: Process documentation, hiring pipelines, critique systems

**Volume:** High (hundreds of candidates)  
**Threat Level:** Low (different positioning)
