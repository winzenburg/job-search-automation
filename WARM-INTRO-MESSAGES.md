## MESSAGING RULES (All Platforms)

### DO
✅ Personalize every message (reference their work, recent announcement, shared connection)  
✅ Be specific (not "I'm interested in AI," but "I'm interested in how Constitutional AI scales to enterprise")  
✅ Lead with value or genuine interest (not "I'm looking for a job")  
✅ Keep initial message short (<5 sentences, 100-150 words max)  
✅ Offer something (insights from your domain, perspective, willingness to help)  
✅ Use their first name (more personal than formal title)  
✅ Send during work hours (8-10 AM or 3-5 PM, Tuesday-Thursday optimal)  

### DON'T
❌ Send generic template messages ("I'm a product leader interested in your company")  
❌ Use multiple exclamation marks (sounds desperate: "I'm so excited!!!")  
❌ Name-drop (unless real connection: avoid "I know [Famous Person]" unless you actually do)  
❌ Hard sell (this is exploratory, not job negotiation)  
❌ Send longer than 150 words in first message (people skim)  
❌ Ask for something before giving something (no "can you introduce me" in cold message)  
❌ Use corporate jargon (be human, be conversational)
